Manifesto

What We Believe

LookTwice exists to slow attention—not as nostalgia, but as practice.

It is a refusal of speed as virtue. A commitment to staying longer than is comfortable.

What this is not

This is not a blog. Not a product. Not a platform.

What this holds

Essays. Observations. Letters to no one in particular.
